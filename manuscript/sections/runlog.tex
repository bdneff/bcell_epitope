\paragraph{Run-script fixes (bringing up run \#1 on Gemini).} Three issues surfaced and were
fixed while getting prep to run; recorded for reproducibility:
\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Module name.} Gemini's module is \texttt{Gromacs} (capital G); lowercase
        \texttt{gromacs} errors on a missing \texttt{shared} dependency. Scripts updated.
  \item \textbf{Config paths under Slurm.} Slurm copies the batch script to a private spool
        dir, so \texttt{\$(dirname \$0)/configs} resolved into the spool dir and \texttt{ions.mdp}
        was not found. Scripts now anchor paths to \texttt{\$SLURM\_SUBMIT\_DIR};
        \textbf{always submit from the repo root}.
  \item \textbf{GPU bonded offload on EM.} \texttt{-bonded gpu} requires a dynamical integrator;
        EM uses \texttt{steep}, so the EM \texttt{mdrun} now uses \texttt{-nb gpu} only. NVT/NPT/
        production use \texttt{md} and keep \texttt{-nb/-pme/-bonded gpu} (audited).
\end{itemize}
Also removed the obsolete \texttt{ns\_type} entry from all mdp files (ignored under the Verlet
scheme). \textbf{Caveat:} Gemini scratch is not durable --- move logs/outputs off scratch after
each run (an earlier prep's logs were lost to a purge). Sanity check per run: \texttt{pdb2gmx.log}
must report \textbf{4 disulfides} for HEL.

\paragraph{Dynamics-feature push (2026-06-30 / 07-01) --- what happened, including the wrong turns.}
Two days of apo-MD feature extraction and scoring against the \emph{energy} label (alanine-scan
$\ddG$), on 6 Tier-1 antigens (\texttt{1AKI 2JEL 1BJ1 1JRH 1AHW 1HGU}; $\sim$9 antibody-epitopes,
$\sim$97 scanned residues). Features computed on Gemini: RMSF, trajectory SASA and SASA fluctuation,
slow-mode (positional-PCA) participation, DCCM coupling, $\phi/\psi/\chi_1$ dihedral flexibility,
per-residue H-bond counts (intra-protein and to water), and gRINN intra-protein interaction energy.
Evaluation: \emph{surface-restricted directional AUROC} --- restrict to surface residues (trajSASA
above the per-antigen median), then $P(\text{feature}_{\text{hotspot}} > \text{feature}_{\text{rest}})$,
reporting the sign; leave-one-antigen-out for generalisation. Under it, raw exposure (SASA) collapses
to $\approx 0.51$ (chance), confirming the baseline the project is built to beat.

\emph{Four bugs surfaced, each of which moved a headline number:} (i) the alanine-scan $\ddG$ table is
in antibody-\emph{complex} PDB numbering while the apo-MD structures are numbered differently --- every
feature$\leftrightarrow\ddG$ join on raw residue number mislabelled 4 of 6 systems (only $39/101$
labelled residues matched by wild-type identity; $101/101$ after per-system offsets
\texttt{1AHW}$-3$, \texttt{1BJ1}$-13$, \texttt{1HGU}$-1$, \texttt{1JRH}$-10$; persisted in
\texttt{benchmark/labels/numbering\_offsets.json} + \texttt{ddg\_by\_md\_residue.csv}, WT-verified).
(ii) gRINN's output is $0$-indexed while every other file is $1$-indexed --- the energy join was off by
one residue; correcting it (\texttt{grinn.resid $+=1$}) \textbf{collapsed interaction-energy detection
$0.67\to0.57$}: the apparent energy signal, and the ``internal-energy / protrusion / under-satisfied
side-chain'' mechanism built on it, were an alignment artefact --- \textbf{that narrative is dead}.
(iii) gRINN for VEGF ran on a single protomer of the obligate homodimer (\texttt{1BJ1}, 94 vs 188
residues) --- energy scale and PC1 broken; \texttt{1BJ1} excluded. (iv) reproducibility gaps: the
reassessment computation lived only in an ephemeral session (committed script was a stub), the two
figures were not regenerable, and the committed trajSASA carried \texttt{NaN} for \texttt{UNK}
residues. A runnable evaluation script now replaces the stub
(\texttt{analysis/reassess\_corrected\_labels.py}): it applies the offsets with a WT-identity assertion,
fixes the gRINN index in one place, and locks the numbers.

\emph{Corrected, reconciled headline} (5 systems, \texttt{1BJ1} dropped; surface, per-antigen AUROC):
H-bonds to water $0.66$ (best single feature, survives every correction), H-bonds intra $0.60$,
trajectory SASA $0.57$, interaction energy (vdW) $0.57$, DCCM $0.56$, backbone flexibility $0.52$,
PC1 participation $0.50$, SASA fluctuation $0.40$. The HB-water \emph{gradient} among labelled residues
is $\rho=-0.33$ (an earlier $-0.50$ was wrongly surface-restricted, dropping \texttt{2JEL}, which runs
the other way, $\rho=+0.27$); it is not unanimous. \textbf{Honest state:} no single antigen-only feature
is a strong predictor of the energy label at $N\approx6$; every exposure/flexibility feature is weak
(as the thesis predicts), and interaction energy is now dead. The real bottleneck is $N$ --- no
transferable linear combination beats the best single feature (LOAO logistic goes below chance) ---
which motivates scaling the label set. \emph{Still open:} reconcile the HB-water detection number
between the runnable script ($\approx0.52$ on the shared surface set) and the Science-kernel $0.656$
(a residual AUROC/negative-set difference); regenerate the feature figures once locked; the
FRESEAN low-frequency-mode run (proper replacement for the PCA placeholder) is in progress.
