\emph{validate the prep$\to$equil$\to$production pipeline on Gemini and extract a first RMSF /
flexibility feature} (the Westhof re-test). House default stack signed off as the starting point.

\paragraph{Stack.} AMBER99SB-ILDN / TIP3P; cubic box, 1.2\,nm clearance; neutralize $+$
0.15\,M NaCl; leapfrog, 2\,fs, LINCS on h-bonds; PME ($r_\mathrm{coul}=r_\mathrm{vdw}=1.0$\,nm,
potential-shift, DispCorr); V-rescale at 300\,K (Protein / Non-Protein); EM $\to$ 100\,ps NVT $\to$
100\,ps NPT (both position-restrained) $\to$ \textbf{100\,ns} production (single trajectory, for
RMSF statistics). Random seeds (\texttt{gen\_seed} $=$ \texttt{ld\_seed} $= -1$): bitwise
reproducibility is not the target --- the workflow reproduces from git-tracked inputs and saved
derived outputs, and random seeds give genuinely independent replicas. Frozen per-run \texttt{.mdp}
files: \texttt{md/1AKI/apo/configs/} (run dirs are organized \texttt{md/<PDB>/<state>/}).

\paragraph{Deliberate deviations from the house scripts.}
(i) \textbf{C-rescale} barostat for NPT equilibration (Parrinello-Rahman only in production), since
PR can ring when started far from equilibrium; (ii) \textbf{cubic} box (the old house scripts
disagreed cubic/dodecahedron) --- chosen for interpretability and uniform across all systems, at a
modest extra-solvent cost vs.\ dodecahedron.

\paragraph{HEL-specific checkpoints (not glossed over).} pdb2gmx must form HEL's \textbf{4 disulfides}
(6--127, 30--115, 64--80, 76--94) --- verify the log reports 4 SS bonds. His15 protonation: record the
assigned state. Crystal waters (78 HOH) removed; fresh TIP3P added. Read every grompp warning rather
than letting \texttt{-maxwarn} hide it.

\paragraph{Caveats / flags.} TIP3P over-mobilizes water and 2\,fs$+$LINCS suppress H dynamics --- fine
for relative RMSF and pipeline validation, \emph{not} for the dynamics-grade features. 100\,ns is a single
trajectory for statistics; replicas are deferred (random seeds make adding independent ones trivial).

\paragraph{Deferred: the distinctive run.} FRESEAN mode participation and 3D-2PT hydration
thermodynamics require a \emph{separate} protocol --- high-frequency velocity output, constraint
removal / sub-fs dt, TIP4P/2005 --- with settings pulled from the FRESEAN and energy-transfer
manuscripts. This is the run that powers the mode-based and hydration feature classes.

\section{Structure completeness and the repair strategy}\label{sec:repair}

Several crystal structures are incomplete --- disordered termini, internal loops, or whole
segments that the experiment never resolved (PDB \texttt{REMARK 465}). Apo-MD demands a chemically
complete chain, so each gap forces a choice. We make that choice explicitly per structure rather
than feeding everything through one tool, because \textbf{the right repair scales with gap length},
and the wrong tool on a long gap produces a confident-looking but meaningless model.

\paragraph{What a ``fixer'' can and cannot supply.} The instinct to ``just grab the FASTA for the
missing residues'' conflates two different problems. The deposited sequence (\texttt{SEQRES}, the
same residues a fixer recovers automatically by diffing \texttt{SEQRES} against the resolved
\texttt{ATOM} records) supplies the \emph{identity} of the missing residues --- but identity is the
easy half. The hard half is \emph{geometry}: where the missing backbone atoms sit in three
dimensions, about which the sequence says nothing. \texttt{pdbfixer} (OpenMM) builds missing
heavy atoms onto resolved residues from templates --- valid and routine --- and threads missing
residues in with default internal coordinates followed by a short minimization of only the new
atoms. That minimization removes clashes; it performs \emph{no} conformational sampling. The result
is therefore reliable for a short surface gap (apo equilibration relaxes a two- or three-residue
loop anyway) and arbitrary for anything longer, where real loop modelling (\textsc{Modeller},
Rosetta) or a full predicted model (AlphaFold) is required. Disordered \emph{termini} are not
modelled at all: they bear no labels and would only add unrestrained flailing, so we cap them.

\paragraph{Per-structure decisions} (gaps read from \texttt{REMARK 465}; verified
2026-06-29). \texttt{pdbfixer} needs Python~$\geq 3.10$, so repairs run in the Gemini environment.
